    Wanna find out if the job posting is real or fake???


    When applying to a new job, many of us wonder if the position applying for is real or fake. While this question may not arise to some before or immediately after applying for the job; but at certain point when you haven't heard back from that Company's HR / Recruiter even though your skills really matched with the job description it may cloud your mind. Finding a job is overall a very stressful process and to ease that a little bit, we created a Machine Learning Model (Natural Language Processing) that will help determine the Job's authenticity by analyzing the job description.
