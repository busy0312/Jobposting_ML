Exploratory Analysis

For our project, we chose a dataset from Kaggle on Real and Fake Job Postings. Using the dataset we preprocessed the dataset prior to fitting the model, performed feature selection and removed any unnecessary features.

Based on our exploratory analysis, Full-time opportunities make up the largest percentage of total posts. 41% of Fake job posts require High-School or equivalent education while 53% of Real posts require a Bachelor’s. 41.5% of Fake jobs posts are Entry-Level while 35.5% of Real job posts are Mid-level 24.2% are Entry-level.

Due to the relatively small difference in these areas, it was decided to use TensorFlow using a text classification model for our Machine Learning Model.

After tuning and comparing the classifiers we concluded that "Binary Classification " gave us maximum accuracy.
